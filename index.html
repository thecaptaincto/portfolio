Hello.

I'm captaincto, a UI/UX designer and developer based in Montréal. I design from scratch and then build it — and I care deeply about the gap between the two. It all started with game modding: making custom maps, then asking why everything looked the way it did, then learning to change it. That curiosity turned into a craft.

I work well solo and async: independent, shipping things from idea to deployed product, comfortable across the whole stack from interface design to backend API. Right now I'm building Touch Grass — a travel planning and outdoor wellness app, solo from concept to launch.
