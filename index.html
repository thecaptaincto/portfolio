Hello!

I'm captaincto, a creative developer based in Montréal who builds things for the web. I got into coding because I wanted to make video games — specifically after getting hooked on Brawl Stars in 2018 and wanting to create my own. That curiosity never went away, and two years in, I'm shipping real projects from scratch.

I work well independently: I'm comfortable communicating async, managing my own schedule, and seeing things through without hand-holding. If you need someone who can pick up a task remotely and ship it without constant check-ins, that's exactly what I do.

Right now I'm at the intermediate level — comfortable enough to build full projects, humble enough to know there's a lot more to learn. I believe in hard work, products that don't suck, and always improving.
