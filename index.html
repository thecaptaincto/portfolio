Hello!

I'm captaincto, an aspiring developer from the heart of the city lights. I'm not a master coder yet—more like an enthusiastic learner with a laptop and a lot of late nights. But I'm hardworking, curious, and I genuinely love what I do.

I grew up surrounded by the warm glow of tradition and the bright buzz of modern technology. That duality—heritage and innovation—shapes how I see the world and how I build things. Every project I touch is a chance to blend old-world craft with new-world possibility.

Right now, I'm at the intermediate level—comfortable enough to ship projects, humble enough to know there's galaxies more to learn. I believe in showing up, putting in the work, and creating things that matter. One line of code at a time.
